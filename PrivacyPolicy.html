Privacy Policy

Penny Flow
Last updated: 2025-09-28

We do not collect, store, or share personal data. All records you create in Penny Flow are saved locally on your device via Apple’s Core Data. Your data never leaves your device unless you choose to export or back it up yourself.

What data we collect

None. The app does not use analytics, advertising, tracking, or third‑party SDKs.

How your data is processed

  • Entries you create (amount, category, note, date, currency) are stored locally on your device.
  • No cloud servers are used by the app.
  • You may delete any entry in the app at any time.

Permissions

The app does not request access to contacts, photos, location, notifications, camera, microphone, or other sensitive device data.

Children’s Privacy

The app is suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Third parties

No third‑party SDKs or services are integrated.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy to reflect product changes or legal requirements. We will revise the “Last updated” date above when changes occur.
